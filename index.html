Welcome to Puzzle Piece, a social platform designed to provide the missing piece in your entrepreneurial puzzle. Do you have an idea? Have you identified a problem that could change the world if solved? This platform builds upon the limitless potential available at almost every collaborative University in the world. Too many entrepreneurial programs provide support only after business plans have been finalized and teams have spent years working together. How are those teams created and how can we help each other craft better business plans and cultivate more promising businesses? So many brilliant ideas are gathering dust on a shelf because something is missing. Well, here is the solution, bring your idea and we will help you build your team.

The Puzzle Piece Platform
Performs 5 Functions
(Click To Discover!)

1. Open a free space for ideas.

Open a free space where entrepreneurs, doctors, scholars, scientists, financiers, artists, and engineers can pose their questions or ideas. The point is you have an idea/question, and you need to take it to the next level, whether in high school or a tenured professor, your voice can be heard.

2. Create Teams and Connect Innovators

Create easily navigable routes to connect an idea with an engineer to implement the design, a business expert to make the product market ready/viable, and a lawyer to facilitate the patent and licensing process. – We will help you build your team.

3. Cultivate a Social Setting.

Cultivate a social setting where brilliant minds can connect with one another and learn from each other’s experience, wisdom and expertise. You can keep up to date on the entrepreneurial world, and can see trends in innovation at their inception. You can attract talent to your start up. Personalize your membership so that you focus on industries you are passionate about.

4. Leverage Human Capital.

a. Building business model templates
b. Providing positive & critical criticism regarding ideas, projects and prototypes.
c. Enhancing the trouble-shooting and de-bugging processes; many hands makes light work

5. Generate Exposure To/For Investors

Provide high level exposure to investors looking to provide the recourses necessary to transform models into real businesses.
